# Cyanoacrylate instillation for occlusion of parotid sinuses

- HealthTech guidance
- Reference number: HTG20
- Published: 
							
								25 February 2004

## Overview

[Evidence-based recommendations](https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/HTG20/chapter/1-Recommendations) on cyanoacrylate instillation for occlusion of parotid sinuses. This involves injecting a solution of lipiodiol and cyanoacrylate via the sinus into the parotid gland, sealing the sinus.

**Last reviewed:** 25 February 2004

Interventional procedures guidance 42 has been migrated to HealthTech guidance 20. The recommendations and accompanying content remain unchanged.
