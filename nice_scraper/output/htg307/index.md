## Overview

<label>Interventional procedures, IPG449 - Issued: March 2013</label>
[Evidence-based recommendations](https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/htg307/chapter/1-Recommendations) on insertion of customised titanium implants, with soft tissue cover, for orofacial reconstruction. This involves inserting titanium implants to replace broken bones in the face as part of orofacial reconstruction.

**Last reviewed:** 01 July 2013

Interventional procedures guidance 449 has been migrated to HealthTech guidance 307. The recommendations and accompanying content remain unchanged.

This guidance and [NICE HealthTech guidance 313](http://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/htg313) together replace the previous NICE interventional procedures guidance on customised titanium implants for orofacial reconstruction (IPG28).

**Next review:** This guidance will be reviewed if there is new evidence or safety concerns.
