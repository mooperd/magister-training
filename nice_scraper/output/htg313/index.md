- HealthTech guidance
- Reference number: HTG313
- Published: 
							
								23 July 2013

## Overview

<label>Interventional procedures, IPG457 - Issued: July 2013</label>
[Evidence-based recommendations](https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/htg313/chapter/1-Recommendations) on insertion of customised exposed titanium implants, without soft tissue cover, for complex orofacial reconstruction. This involves making the titanium implants specially to fit the person using a model of their face.

**Last reviewed:** 23 July 2013

Interventional procedures guidance 457 has been migrated to HealthTech guidance 313. The recommendations and accompanying content remain unchanged.

This guidance and [NICE's HealthTech guidance 307](https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/htg307) together replace the previous NICE interventional procedures guidance on customised titanium implants for orofacial reconstruction (IPG28).

**Next review:** This guidance will be reviewed if there is new evidence or safety concerns.
